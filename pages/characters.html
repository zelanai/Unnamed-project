Sylvie

Sylvie seems to hate the world, making the worst of every situation. She's a seasoned adventurer, having been a member of the illustrious Flaming Shield Adventuring Co. but has little love for the craft these days. Despite her cynical outlook, Sylvie is always there to help the wounded with a potion or poultice of her own creation, refusing to leave a person in need when she can help.
